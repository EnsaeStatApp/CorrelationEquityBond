Here is an explanation of all the macroeconomic variables considered : (the source FRED is the Federal Reserve of St Louis


1 - Real Activity :

INDPRO : Industrial Production Index source : FRED

USGDP : Nominal GDP / Real GDP / GDP Deflator / Population / Nominal GDP per capita / Real GDP per capita (Source : MeasuringWorth)

Remarque : Unemployement Rate only starts at 1948 which is too late to be considered here (or shall we restrict our analysis starting this year ??)

UNRATE : Unemployement Rate (BLS : Bureau of Labor Statistics)

USRECD : NBER based Recession Indicators for the United States from the Period following the Peak through the Trough (Source : FRED)

TCMILBSNNCB : Nonfinancial Corporate Business; Debt Securities and Loans; Liability, Level // le niveau total d’endettement (en dollars courants) des entreprises non financières américaines

TCU : Manufacturing Capacity Utilization


2 - Prices and Inflation

PPIACO : Producer Price Index All Commodities (FRED)

HP : Home Prices (FRED)

GP : Gold Prices (FRED)

CPI : Consumer Price Index (FRED)

3 - Banques

M2SL : Masse Monétaire

DGS10 : Taux d’intérêt à long terme (ex : rendement des obligations d’État à 10 ans)

TB3MS : 3-Month Treasury Bill Rate
